Elevator Pitch

For any ordinary User
who buys and stores food at home
the 'Kitchen App'
is a web application
which enables to list any ingredients stored at home.

Unlike the current situation of producing food wastage beacause of the lack of information about the quantity of ingredients at home
our product will help to handle the 'stock-control' at any home
provides better overview of your ingredients at home 
in this way helps to reduce food wastage caused by overconsuming.

(Later this application will broaden with useful functions, like matching your ingredients to recepies,
list and store your own recepies,
helps to sort ingredients according to food allergens, 
gives hints and tips to make your time spend in your kitchen easier and more pleasant)
